Maintenance Tips (Better Cooling & Longer Life)

  • Defrost your fridge regularly if it’s a manual-defrost model
  • Keep fridge vents clear for proper air circulation
  • Do not overload with warm food; allow cooling balance
  • Clean condenser coils every 6 months for efficiency
  • Check and replace door gaskets if not sealing properly

FAQs

Why is my fridge not cooling properly?

Common causes include gas leakage, dirty condenser coils, faulty thermostat, or compressor issues. Our technicians inspect and fix the root cause.

How often should I service my refrigerator?

Preventive maintenance once a year helps ensure efficient cooling and prevents breakdowns, especially before summer.

Is gas refilling safe for refrigerators?

Yes, when done by certified professionals with genuine refrigerants. We ensure leak testing and warranty-backed refilling.

What are the service charges?

We charge a nominal inspection fee. Specific repairs (gas filling, compressor, spares) are quoted separately and started only after approval.
