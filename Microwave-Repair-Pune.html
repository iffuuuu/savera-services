Tips for Maintaining Your Microwave Oven

  • Clean microwave interior regularly to prevent food buildup
  • Do not use metal utensils inside the microwave
  • Avoid running the microwave empty
  • Check door seals to ensure safety
  • Get professional servicing once a year
