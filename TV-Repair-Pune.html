Common Problems We Fix

  • No picture, blank screen, or dim display
  • No sound or distorted audio
  • TV not turning on / stuck on standby
  • Backlight failure or flickering screen
  • Lines, spots, or cracks on display panel
  • Smart TV apps not working or Wi-Fi not connecting
  • Remote not working, IR sensor faults

Maintenance Tips (Better Viewing & Longer Life)

  • Keep TV away from direct sunlight and moisture
  • Use a voltage stabilizer to protect from power surges
  • Clean screen with microfiber cloth only, avoid harsh liquids
  • Update Smart TV software regularly
  • Turn off TV completely when not in use for long hours

FAQs

Why is my TV screen blank but sound is working?

This is usually a backlight or display panel issue. Our technicians can replace backlights or repair the panel as needed.

Can a cracked TV screen be repaired?

Minor internal lines can be fixed, but for physical cracks panel replacement is required. We provide genuine parts with warranty.

My Smart TV apps are not loading, what should I do?

It may be due to outdated software or Wi-Fi issues. We handle firmware upgrades, Wi-Fi module repairs, and software fixes.

What are the service charges?

We charge a small inspection fee. Any specific repair (panel, motherboard, backlight) is quoted separately after diagnosis.
